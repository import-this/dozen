Controls

Keyboard (Recommended)

Arrow keys / ASD to move the falling tile.
Up arrow / W for a hard drop.

Mouse

Click and drag your mouse left, right or down to move the falling tile.
Double click for a hard drop.

Touch (Mobile)

Drag your finger left, right or down to move the falling tile.
Double tap for a hard drop.

Speeds

Classic

Tiles fall faster as you level up.

Fast

Tiles fall at the same fast pace.

Slow

Tiles fall at the same slow pace.

Practice

Tiles do not fall on their own.
